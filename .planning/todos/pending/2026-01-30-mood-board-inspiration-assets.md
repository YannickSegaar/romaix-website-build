## Problem

Need a place to collect and reference website inspirations and design assets that capture the desired aesthetic.

## Solution

Create section in .planning or similar to store:
- Screenshots of websites with nice designs
- Specific elements that look good (backgrounds, animations, layouts)
- Color/style references
- Can reference during implementation to guide design decisions

This is a running collection to inform design choices.
