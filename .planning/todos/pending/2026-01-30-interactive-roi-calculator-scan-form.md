## Problem

Need an interactive, multi-step animated form where visitors can request a free business scan. Currently have a basic assessment form but want something more engaging with step-by-step animations showing progress.

## Solution

Create animated multi-step form with:
- Step animations showing progress through form
- Required fields: company name, email, contact info, notes/description, website URL
- Optional fields: LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook
- Final step shows scan preview/confirmation they'll receive results
- Consider ROI calculator component that shows potential value

Form should feel premium and interactive, not like a standard contact form.
